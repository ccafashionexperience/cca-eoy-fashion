CCA Spring 2021 Fashion Experience 春天 时尚 经验

Enter

Featuring work by 出色的作品 Kexin Sun Emma Bjorklund Mooni Wang Robert Whitehurst Siti Li Sojung Kim Wenyu Cheng Xiaohan Wu Xiaomeng Li Zhonghui Liu

We are starting to emerge from a collective experience where the whole world was ‘thrown up’ and unsettled. This time has revealed deep fissures in our social fabric...and a clear need for new global visions and collaborations.

Abruptly halted in place as we were, these times also demanded that we slow, observe, reflect, act.

This thesis year was an almost completely remote experience. Besides the usual herculean task of moving from deep conceptual development to 3D garments, additional effort had to be made just to connect, communicate and craft.

Everyone, students and instructors alike, developed new skills and support systems on the fly.

From using i-phones and zoom to conduct fittings in living rooms and bedrooms across the world, to deploying family members as models…… ’the show must go on’ imperative prevailed.

So, these young designers have developed skills particular to this time. Fortitude, resilience and multi-dimensional awareness, that will serve them well in life. They turned inward by necessity, survived and now present their outwardly facing perspectives and ambitions.

If I were to pick one overriding meta theme for this year’s cohort I would say it is tenderness. We are in tender times.

other meta themes include:

anxiety and exhaustion
loneliness
Empathy for the self…. … for the ‘other’ ..and for all species
Vulnerability, courage
Centering ‘Home’ whilst on the move
Local design-sheds
Gender freedom
Creativity and craft as a sanctuary
The power within
Magic
Celebration
a merging of tactile, digital, biological and virtual realms
with that

I’d like everyone to take a breath hold these themes throughout the day bring your own reflections to them and enjoy the fresh visions of CCA’s 2021 graduating fashion design cohort.
